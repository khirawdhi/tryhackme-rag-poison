#vpn_access

- VPN access requires an active user account and MFA.
- If VPN access fails, verify device time and network connectivity.
- Ensure the correct MFA method is selected.
- Retry authentication after confirming MFA approval.
- If access issues persist, contact the IT Helpdesk.
- The Helpdesk may grant temporary break-glass access.
- Break-glass access requires manager approval.
- Do not disable MFA to resolve access issues.
- Do not create shared accounts for VPN access.
