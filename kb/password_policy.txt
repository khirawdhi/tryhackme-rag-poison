#Password_policy

- All user accounts must use unique passwords.
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for VPN and administrative access.
- Passwords must not be shared with other users.
- One-time passwords or MFA codes must not be disclosed.
- Temporary credentials must be approved by the IT Helpdesk.
- Emergency access requests require a documented ticket.
- Emergency access is time-limited and monitored.
- MFA must not be disabled as a workaround.
- Suspected credential compromise must be reported immediately.
